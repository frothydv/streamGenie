How it works

Info where you need it,
exactly when you need it.

Stream Genie reads video pixels under your cursor. When it recognizes something the community has annotated, it tells you what it knows.

1

Open a stream

Stream Genie detects the game from the Twitch category and loads the right community profile automatically.

2

Hover over things

Move your cursor over items in the video — cards, relics, icons, weapons, anything the community has tagged.

3

Get the answer

A popup appears near your cursor with the community's annotation. Move away and it disappears.

4

Zero setup

Nothing to configure. The streamer doesn't install a thing. Works on VODs too.

Community-built

Know something?
Add it.

Stream Genie is the framework. You and your community build the knowledge. Adding a trigger takes 30 seconds — freeze the frame, drag a box, write a description, submit.

Submissions go to GitHub as pull requests. Profile owners merge them. Every viewer sees your annotation the moment it lands.

👁️

Viewers

Hover and learn. No setup needed. Works on any stream with a community profile.

📡

Streamers

Declare a default profile. Your viewers get curated annotations automatically.

🛠️

Contributors

Add triggers for any game. Trusted contributors skip the review step entirely.

Install

Get Stream Genie

Free, open source, Chrome only for now.

Chrome Web Store listing coming soon — sideload for now
1

Download stream-genie-v0.9.zip and unzip it anywhere.

2

Open Chrome, go to chrome://extensions/, and enable Developer mode.

3

Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped extension/ folder.

4

The lamp icon appears in your toolbar. Open a Twitch stream and hover.
